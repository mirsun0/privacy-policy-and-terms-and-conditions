Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how personal data is handled by our private digital notebook and SMS-based automated capture system.

1. Information Collected and Scope

This system is an exclusively private utility designed for personal workflow organization, rapid note-taking, and time-management reminders. The only data collected, processed, or stored is the mobile phone number and the text-based message content voluntarily transmitted by the primary user for note archiving.

2. Strict Non-Sharing of Mobile Data

Jude Gladden Sole Proprietor does not share, sell, or otherwise provide your mobile phone number, opt-in data, or messaging consent information to any third parties, affiliates, or partners for marketing or promotional purposes under any circumstances.

3. Data Storage, Hosting, and Security

All incoming text communications are safely routed and stored locally on private, self-hosted network-attached storage (NAS) architecture. We utilize secure local networks and industry-standard encryption protocols to maintain complete local data ownership and prevent unauthorized access.

4. Message Frequency

Message frequency is entirely user-dependent. Outbound text messages from this system are strictly non-promotional and will only be sent to your device in direct response to an active, user-initiated request—such as a scheduled task notification, log confirmation, or system alert.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this operational privacy policy. Any changes to data handling protocols will be published instantly to this public URL.
